I am a PhD student in the School of Computer Science at the University of St Andrews, and member of the System Research Group.

My research interest


I am interest in areas such as: mobile big data, data distribution, social data analysis, wireless and wired networks, mobile cloud computing, and power saving techniques.


I am researching under the supervision of Dr. Alexander Voss and Dr. Ishbel Duncan in whether a alternative future internet architecture such as Name Data based Networking protocol can be applied in Mobila Ad-hoc networks.
