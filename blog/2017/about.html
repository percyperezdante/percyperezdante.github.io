Current Activities

I am a PhD student in the School of Computer Science at the University of St Andrews, and member of the System Research Group.

My research topic is focus on nMANET, which is an intend to whether an alternative future internet architecture such as Name Data based Networking protocol can be applied in Mobila Ad-hoc networks. Additionally, nMANET was proposed as an alternative network communication architecture to solve the limitations of MANETs in emergency scenarios. I am designing, implementing and testing JNFD, which is the prototype of nMANET, to be functional in Andriod and Linux base mobile devices.

I am working in my researh topic under the supervision of Dr. Alexander Voss and Dr. Ishbel Duncan
